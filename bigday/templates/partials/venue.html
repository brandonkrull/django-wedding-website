The Colony House

The Colony House is located in the heart of Anaheim, about a mile north of Disneyland; it is smack-dab in the middle of the 5-91-57 freeways.

For out-of-towners, the nearest airport is John Wayne (SNA). Flights into SNA are a bit more expensive, but the convenience almost always outweighs the additional cost. If you decide otherwise, LAX is a mere 35 miles away, but be weary of the infamous LA traffic.

Lyft/Uber are readily available for around-town travel.



Where can I stay?
